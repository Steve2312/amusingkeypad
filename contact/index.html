I'm experiencing double taps, what do I do?

You can change the debounce of the keypad using the AmusingKeypad application. Around 50 should be fine.

KeypadV3 cursor auto moves to top left on Windows 10, How to fix this?

Find "VID_001C&PID_100B&MI_02" from all mouse devices and disable it.
